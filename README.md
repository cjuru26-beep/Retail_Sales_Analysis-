# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Business Objective
Analyze retail sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing regions, categories, and customers. Demonstrates skills in SQL, Python, data visualization, and business insights.

## Tools & Skills
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- SQL (pandasql)
- Excel
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning, Analysis, and Visualization
- Business Insights Reporting

## Dataset
Includes order info, customer, product, sales, and region details. CSV file: `train.csv`.

## Analysis Performed
1. Total Sales by Region
2. Total Sales by Category & Sub-Category
3. Monthly Sales Trend
4. Top 10 Customers
5. Regional & Category Breakdown

## Key Insights
- West region highest sales
- Technology category highest revenue
- Q4 is peak sales season
- Top 10 customers contribute ~25% of total sales
